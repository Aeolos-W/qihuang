🏔️ 新安医学

新安医学发源于古徽州（今黄山地区），始于宋，鼎盛于明清，有记载名医466人，医著355部。以"固本培元""营卫一气说"为特色，是中医史上的璀璨明珠。
Xin'an Medicine originated in ancient Huizhou (Huangshan area), began in the Song Dynasty, flourished in Ming and Qing, with 466 recorded physicians and 355 medical texts. Its core theories include "consolidating the root" and "unified defensive and nutritive Qi."

汪昂与《汤头歌诀》

汪昂是明末清初新安医家，他将常用方剂编成歌诀，朗朗上口，至今仍是中医启蒙经典。

Wang Ang was a Xin'an physician who turned prescriptions into easy-to-memorize rhymes, still used today as TCM classics.

固本培元派

新安医学注重先天之本肾和后天之本脾的调养，强调"正气存内，邪不可干"。

Xin'an Medicine emphasizes nourishing the kidneys (congenital root) and spleen (acquired root), highlighting "vital Qi inside, evil cannot invade."
